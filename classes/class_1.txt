attendance (float)

skip_class (modifies the decimal value for the attendance attribute)
pass_classes (modifies grade_level attribute, for example, changing the value from freshman to sophomore)
withdraw (modifies enrollment_status attribute, from enrolled to not)
miss_payment (modifies the good_financial_attribute, true to false)
